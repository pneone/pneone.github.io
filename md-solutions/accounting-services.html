Accounting Services

Accounting, bookkeeping, payroll and financial reporting are integral parts of any business. They provide critical information that can be used to make sound business decisions. Without accurate and up-to-date financial information, it would be difficult to gauge the health of your business and make informed decisions about its future.

Our team of experienced CFO, Controller, and accountants can take financial management responsibilities off your hands. We will work with you to develop a customized accounting solution that meets your specific needs. Whether you need assistance with payroll, bookkeeping, accounting, budgeting, or forecasting, we can help. Let us handle your finances so you can focus on running your business. Contact us today to learn more about our accounting services.

Payroll Service
  • Payroll software setup
  • W-2s, W-3s and W-4s forms
  • Setup Weekly/Bi-Weekly/Monthly payroll runs
  • Calculating Annual/Sick leaves and Vacation pay
  • Calculation of Payroll Withholding
Our cost-effective payroll services relieve your office of the time-consuming task of payroll processing. It's an extension of the financial reporting services we offer on a regular basis. Allow us to aid you in making better use of your business day by supporting you with this important yet time-consuming chore.

Payroll services include access to a web portal 24 hours a day, 7 days a week. Employees may view their pay stubs and W2s without having to wait for you to transmit them. With each payroll run, you, as the employer, get access to payroll reports. We ensure that your payroll taxes are paid correctly and on schedule to the appropriate tax agencies. Payroll is no longer a time-consuming task with Med Financial Solutions.
