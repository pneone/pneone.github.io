Credentialing and Payer Enrollment

Health plans often require credentialing for providers in order to be reimbursed for services. credentialing can be a time-consuming process, but specialists can help streamline the process. They can also help with enrolling with insurance companies and setting up electronic payments.

Credentialing is a time-consuming procedure that, if done incorrectly, may cost your firm thousands of dollars in lost revenue. We handle the whole credentialing process for you, ensuring that your practice is certified accurately and swiftly. Relieving staff and providers of the time-consuming tasks of enrolling and re-credentialing with both government and commercial payers.

Provider Credentialing Services:

  • Group application enrollments to Medicare & Medicaid
  • Payor provider enrollments
  • Application completion and follow -up
  • Works closely with our billing team to monitor any denials and communicates with payers for prompt resolution
