Medical Billing

If you're like most medical practices, you're always looking for ways to generate more revenue. One of the best ways to do this is to focus on denial management and follow ups. If you're not doing them properly, you're leaving money on the table.

At Med Financial Solutions, we specialize in denial management and follow ups. We know how to appeal denials and get your claims paid in a timely fashion. We also know how to follow up on unpaid claims so you don't have to worry about them.

Contact us today and let us help you get the most out of your billing process. We'll help you increase your revenue and get your claims paid in a timely fashion.

  • Insurance Verification

    Prior to submitting a claim, the patient's insurance eligibility is verified. Payments are not delayed as a result of verification.

  • Hospital Relationship

    We'll coordinate data transfers with the hospital's IT department. Collect any charts that are missing.

  • Charge Entry

    In preparation for claims filing, all charges are entered into billing software.

  • Denial Management

    Resolving issues by contacting insurance companies and refiling claims or charging patients for deductibles and co-pays.

  • Claims follow-up

    All unpaid claims will be followed up on by our billing staff.

  • Payment Posting

    To make the revenue cycle efficient, remittances for payments and rejections are posted daily.

  • Claim Submission

    Our billing platform is capable of electronically submitting 95% of claims to each healthcare plan.

  • Chart Reconciliation

    Missing charts are a constant problem for hospital-based organizations. We'll work with hospital staff to get the information we need to do a reconciliation and find the missing records.

  • Patient Statements

    We can provide patient statements via text message, e-mail or traditional mail.

  • Reporting

    Performance reports are updated on a daily basis to reflect the most recent data. Custom reports are available.

  • Collection Agency

    We'll send your claims to the collection agency of your choosing.
