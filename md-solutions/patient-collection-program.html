Patient Collection Program

Patient Collection Follow-up Approach

We have a unique experience team that deals with Patient Collections. With our program we minimize patient compliments and make this more of a positive experience for each patient. All patients need is a little assistance.

  1. 01
    Our calls to patients start by calling on behalf of the physician’s group/Hospital to cut through any confusion and stay out of collections;
  2. 02
    We take the time to explain how billing works. For Example, clarify that hospitals only bill for their facilities and supplies, while physicians’ groups bill separately for the time and effort of the medical professionals who provided their care;
  3. 03
    We can refer patients to their insurance company for questions about their balance, but also invite them to pay their portion of the bill over the phone.
